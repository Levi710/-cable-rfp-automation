## PRODUCT OVERVIEW

Polycab's 33 kV XLPE (Cross-Linked Polyethylene) insulated power cables are designed for underground transmission and distribution networks. These cables offer superior electrical, mechanical, and thermal properties suitable for demanding applications.

**Key Features**:
- XLPE insulation for enhanced electrical properties
- Steel wire armoring for mechanical protection
- Compliant with IS 7098 (Part 2) and IEC 60502-2
- Suitable for underground installation
- Long service life (30+ years)
- Environmentally friendly

## CONSTRUCTION DETAILS

### Cable Structure (From Inner to Outer)

1. **Conductor**: 120 sq.mm copper stranded
2. **Conductor Screen**: Semi-conducting XLPE, 0.8mm
3. **Insulation**: XLPE 5.5mm per core
4. **Insulation Screen**: Semi-conducting XLPE, 0.8mm
5. **Metallic Screen**: Copper tape/wire, 16 sq.mm
6. **Filler**: PP/PVC filler for roundness
7. **Inner Bedding**: PVC ST1, 1.8mm
8. **Armoring**: 2 layers galvanized steel wire, 2.5mm each
9. **Outer Sheath**: PVC ST2, 3.0mm
