## SECTION 4: TESTING AND ACCEPTANCE REQUIREMENTS

### 4.1 Routine Tests (On Every Drum/Reel)

1. **Conductor Resistance Test**
   - DC resistance measurement at 20°C
   - As per IS 7098 Clause 12.2

2. **High Voltage Withstand Test**
   - Test voltage: 70 kV AC for 5 minutes
   - As per IS 7098 Clause 12.3

3. **Insulation Resistance Test**
   - Minimum 10,000 MΩ.km at 20°C
   - As per IS 7098 Clause 12.4

4. **Visual Inspection**
   - Dimensional verification
   - Marking and identification check
   - Packaging integrity

### 4.2 Type Tests (Once per Product Type)

1. **Voltage Test**
   - AC voltage withstand: 70 kV for 30 minutes
   - Partial discharge test at 1.5 times operating voltage

2. **Mechanical Tests**
   - Tensile strength of conductor
   - Elongation at break
   - Bending test (15D minimum radius)
   - Impact test at -10°C

3. **Thermal Tests**
   - Heat cycle test (20 cycles at 90°C)
   - Short circuit test (250°C for 5 seconds)
   - Ageing test (240 hours at 135°C)

4. **Environmental Tests**
   - Water penetration test
   - Flame propagation test
   - Smoke density test (for LSZH)
   - Corrosion resistance of armoring

5. **Electrical Tests**
   - Partial discharge measurement
   - Dielectric loss tangent (tan δ)
   - Capacitance measurement
   - Lightning impulse test (170 kV)

### 4.3 Sample Tests

Random samples (2% of supply) shall be tested at:
- **CPRI, Bangalore** or
- **NABL accredited laboratory**

Tests as per IS 7098 complete suite.

### 4.4 Acceptance Criteria

- **Routine Tests**: 100% pass rate required
- **Type Tests**: All tests must pass as per IS 7098
- **Sample Tests**: Maximum 1 failure allowed (re-test permitted)

**Rejection**: Any batch failing routine tests will be rejected and replaced at supplier's cost.
