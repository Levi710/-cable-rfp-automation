## SECTION 2: SCOPE OF WORK

### 2.1 Materials to be Supplied

#### Item 1: 11 kV XLPE Armored Underground Cable

**Quantity**: 350 Kilometers (Three Hundred Fifty Kilometers)

**Technical Specifications**:

| S.No | Parameter | Specification |
|------|-----------|--------------|
| 1 | Voltage Rating | 11 kV (Medium Voltage) |
| 2 | Conductor | Electrolytic Grade Copper, Stranded Class 2 |
| 3 | Conductor Size | 35 sq.mm minimum |
| 4 | Number of Cores | 3-core |
| 5 | Conductor Screen | Semi-conducting extruded layer |
| 6 | Insulation | Cross-Linked Polyethylene (XLPE) |
| 7 | Insulation Screen | Semi-conducting extruded layer |
| 8 | Metallic Screen | Copper wires/tape, min 16 sq.mm |
| 9 | Bedding | PVC compound, 1.8mm min |
| 10 | Armoring | Double layer Galvanized Steel Wire |
| 11 | Outer Sheath | PVC/LSZH, 2.5mm min |
| 12 | Color | Black outer sheath |
| 13 | Standards | IS 7098 (Part 1):2011, IEC 60502-1 |

**Design Parameters**:
- Maximum Conductor Temperature: 90°C (continuous)
- Short Circuit Temperature: 250°C (max 5 sec)
- Rated Voltage (U0/U): 6/10 kV, 11 kV system
- Frequency: 50 Hz
- Installation: Underground in ducts/trenches

**Electrical Characteristics**:
- AC Voltage Test: 26 kV for 5 minutes (routine)
- Insulation Resistance: Min 500 MΩ.km at 20°C
- Partial Discharge: < 10 pC at 1.73 U0
- Capacitance: As per IS 7098

## SECTION 3: TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

### 3.1 Standards and Codes

All materials shall conform to:
- **Indian Standards**: IS 7098 (Part 1):2011
- **International Standards**: IEC 60502-1
- **Quality Standards**: ISO 9001:2015
- **Environmental**: ISO 14001:2015

### 3.2 Design Requirements

**Cable Construction**:
1. Conductor: Compacted stranded copper, Class 2 as per IS 8130
2. Conductor Screen: Extruded semi-conducting compound
3. Insulation: XLPE compound, min 3.4mm thickness
4. Insulation Screen: Bonded semi-conducting layer
5. Metallic Screen: Copper wire screen with PVC separator
6. Inner Sheath: PVC bedding over screen
7. Armoring: Round galvanized steel wires (2 layers)
8. Outer Sheath: ST2 type PVC or LSZH compound

**Marking**:
- Cable identification every 1 meter
- Manufacturer name, year of manufacture, IS marking
- Sequential length marking

## SECTION 4: QUALITY ASSURANCE & TESTING

### 4.1 Type Tests (Pre-qualification)

Type test certificate from CPRI/ERDA or equivalent NABL accredited lab required for:

1. **Electrical Tests**
   - AC voltage withstand test
   - Partial discharge measurement
   - Hot set test for insulation
   - Measurement of capacitance
   - Measurement of electric resistance of conductor
   - Measurement of insulation resistance

2. **Mechanical and Physical Tests**
   - Tensile strength and elongation of copper
   - Measurement of thickness of insulation and sheath
   - Hot set test for sheath
   - Pressure test at high temperature
   - Bending test
   - Impact test
   - Shrinkage test

3. **Ageing Tests**
   - Ageing of insulation (air oven method)
   - Ageing of sheath

4. **Special Tests**
   - Lightning impulse voltage test
   - Switching impulse test (if applicable)
   - Measurement of dielectric loss factor

### 4.2 Routine Tests (100% Production)

On every drum:
1. Conductor resistance measurement
2. High voltage test (26 kV for 5 minutes)
3. Partial discharge test
4. Insulation resistance test
5. Dimension check and visual inspection

### 4.3 Sample Tests

NTPC reserves right to draw samples (2-5% of supply) for testing at:
- CPRI Bangalore
- ERDA Vadodara  
- Any NABL accredited laboratory

Tests as per complete IS 7098 test schedule.

### 4.4 Inspection

- **100% inspection** at manufacturer's works by NTPC representative
- Third-party inspection mandatory
- Joint inspection before dispatch
